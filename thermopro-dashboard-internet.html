⚠ Strapping pins — GPIO 2 (boot mode), GPIO 8 (onboard LED, active LOW), GPIO 9 (BOOT button). State may fluctuate during reset/bootloader — avoid for critical I/O.
Interfaces — SPI: GPIO 4 (SCK), 5 (MISO), 6 (MOSI), 7 (SS) · I2C: GPIO 8 (SDA), 9 (SCL) · UART: GPIO 20 (RX), 21 (TX). All remappable via ESP32 mux.
ADC — 6 channels (12-bit SAR): GPIO 0–5 (ADC1_CH0–CH5) · 11× GPIO (PWM) · BLE 5.0 · WiFi b/g/n · Deep sleep ~43μA
